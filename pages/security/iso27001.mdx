# ISO 27001 Certification

Langfuse has achieved ISO 27001 certification, demonstrating our commitment to information security management.

## What is ISO 27001?

ISO 27001 is an internationally recognized standard for information security management systems (ISMS). It provides a framework for organizations to identify, analyze, and address information security risks.

## Our Certification

Langfuse has successfully implemented an Information Security Management System (ISMS) that complies with the ISO 27001 standard. Our certification covers all core processes related to the development and operation of the Langfuse platform.

The ISO 27001 certificate is available upon request to customers on Pro and Enterprise plans. To request access to the certificate, please visit our [security documents request page](/request-security-docs).

## Benefits for Our Customers

Our ISO 27001 certification provides our customers with:

- Assurance that we follow industry best practices for information security
- Confidence that we have a comprehensive approach to identifying and addressing security risks
- Evidence of our commitment to continuous improvement in security controls
- Documentation to help meet your own compliance requirements

For more information about our ISO 27001 certification or other compliance measures, please contact us at compliance@langfuse.com.
