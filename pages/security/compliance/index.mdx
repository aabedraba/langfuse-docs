# Compliance Overview

Langfuse is committed to maintaining the highest standards of security and compliance. We have obtained several certifications and comply with various regulatory frameworks to ensure the protection of your data.

## Our Certifications and Compliance Standards

Langfuse has achieved the following certifications and compliance standards:

- **SOC2 Type II**: We have successfully completed the SOC 2 Type II audit, demonstrating our commitment to security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy.

- **ISO 27001**: We have implemented an Information Security Management System (ISMS) that complies with the ISO 27001 standard, covering all core processes related to the development and operation of the Langfuse platform.

- **HIPAA**: We are compliant with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and can provide a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) for healthcare organizations.

- **GDPR**: We are committed to compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), implementing comprehensive measures to protect the privacy rights of individuals within the European Union and the European Economic Area.

## Requesting Compliance Documentation

Compliance documentation such as SOC2 reports and ISO 27001 certificates are available upon request to customers on Pro and Enterprise plans. To request access to these documents, please visit our [security documents request page](/request-security-docs).

For more information about our compliance measures, please contact us at compliance@langfuse.com.
