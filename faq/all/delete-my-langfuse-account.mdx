# How can I delete my Langfuse account?

To delete your Langfuse account, please follow these steps:

1. Compose an email from the email address you used to sign up for your Langfuse account.
2. Address the email to support@langfuse.com.
3. Clearly state your request for account deletion in the email body.
4. Send the email.

Our support team will process your request, and you will be notified once your account has been deleted.
