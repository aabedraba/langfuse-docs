# How can I delete my Langfuse account?

To delete your Langfuse account, please follow these steps:

1. Compose an email from the address you used to sign up for Langfuse.
2. Address the email to support@langfuse.com.
3. Request the deletion of your account in your email message.

Once your request is received, the support team will process it in accordance with their policies.
